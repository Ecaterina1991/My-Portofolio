Hi, I'm Ecaterina!

I grew up loving beauty in all its forms and having an innate intuition for harmony. In every project I put passion, finding the middle key between what the user wants and the best form of presentation in the world of Web Development and Web Design.

I initially pursued a career in pharmacy, but my love for technology and the beauty led me to explore the world of web development and design, which allows me to combine my passion for creating visually appealing designs with my desire to improve the way people interact with technology. To further develop my skills, I completed the Google IT Support Specialist and Google UX Designer courses, and now I am excited to apply my knowledge to real-world projects. In addition to my passion for technology, I am also an avid painter, a lover of literature and swimming. I believe that my diverse interests and background have helped me develop a unique perspective that I bring to my work. I am always looking for ways to improve and evolve as a designer. Thank you for visiting! I hope my portfolio provides a glimpse into my approach to design and the value I can bring to your project.
